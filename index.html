AIRBNB + History and Florentine Steak at the Birthplace of Ezio Marchi

My name is Anna and I am offering a unique experience that takes place in the birthplace of Ezio Marchi, the so-called 'father of Chianina,' who was the first to scientifically study the cattle breed in Italy. As a result of his work, today the 'bistecca alla Fiorentina' has become famous worldwide. You can enjoy a renowned steak for dinner and the story of his life and legacy, and you can also spend the night in the house where he was born and where his veterinarian father worked. Together, we can visit a Chianina cattle farm, where we will purchase our steaks from a specialized butcher. I will prepare a dinner for you that will be accompanied by plenty of local organic red wine and will conclude with delicious sweets and Vin Santo. It will be a true culinary and cultural journey into the heart of Tuscan tradition.
